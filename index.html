01. About Us

Built By Investors
For Investors.

Trading Vision is built by investors and for investors. Users can use token to vote on the future of Trading Vision. Proposals will be published on Snapshot, and will relate to the future development and expansion of the platform.

At Trading Vision, we strive to develop a platform that is useful enough for professional investors and intuitive enough for first-time users.

TVT is the native token of the Trading Vision Platform, designed for service and transaction fees, liquidity, and rewards. Every TVT holder will be able to submit and vote on governance proposals directly from their wallet, helping steer the future of the Trading Vision Platform.
